JAWATANKUASA PERWAKILAN NON RESIDEN UiTM CAWANGAN SELANGOR

JPNRUCS

Jawatankuasa Perwakilan Non-Residen (JPNR) UiTM is a student body established to represent and advocate for the welfare of Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) students who do not live in the university dormitory (non-resident). These students usually rent houses or rooms off campus.

Roles and Responsibilities of JPNR:

  1. Representing Non-Resident Students:

    Act as a liaison between the university and non-resident students.

    Presenting issues or problems faced by non-resident students to the university.
  2. Student Welfare:

    Ensure that the basic needs of non-resident students such as safety, transportation facilities, and housing are met.

    Providing support in the form of welfare assistance if needed.
  3. Program Organization:

    Planning and implementing programs to improve the interaction and welfare of non-resident students.

    For example, safety awareness programs, community activities, and self-development workshops.
  4. Safety and Welfare:

    Helping to ensure that non-resident students live in a safe environment.

    Acting as an intermediary if an incident occurs that requires the attention of the university.
  5. Information and Assistance:

    Provides important information to non-resident students, such as university transportation, emergency procedures, and campus activities.

Structure of the JPNR:
- President
- Vice President
- Secretary
- Vice Secretary
- Treasurer
- Vice Treasurer
Committee Members (AJK) for various portfolios such as security, transportation, programs, and welfare.

JPNR plays an important role in ensuring that non-resident students receive the same attention and support as students living in university dormitories.
